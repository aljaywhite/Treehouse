The Ultimate Quiz Challenge

Welcome to the Batman Actor and Title Game!

Introduction:

  • Using the provided clues, figure out which actor played Batman.
  • After each actors name is provided, you will have the option to name the title of the film that clue pertained to.
  • Each correct name is worth 2 points. However each incorrect name will take 2 point away from your total score. (Spelling counts!)
  • Each correct bonus is worth 1 point. Getting a bonus point incorrect or skipping it by pressing OK will not hurt your total score.
  • Bonus answers must be full and correct to be awarded points. E.G. Casino Royale

Rules:

  • 1st. Name the actor (whole name, firstname, or last name).
  • 2nd. BONUS! Name the film. (Optional and will have no negative effect on score) Simply hit OK to continue.
